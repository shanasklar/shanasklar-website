Meet Shana

I learned the hard way that knowing isn't the same as feeling safe.

I spent eight years in medicine. I understood how the body worked, how systems worked, how to show up for other people. What I didn't understand was how to feel safe in my own life — financially, personally, or otherwise.

When I started studying nervous system regulation, everything clicked. The reason people avoid their finances isn't laziness — it's survival mode. The reason dogs act out isn't stubbornness — it's that their human is dysregulated and they feel it.

The Common Ground is built on that insight. I'm a certified Scentsible K9 trainer working with dogs and their humans in Meridian, Idaho. I'm also a financial educator and coach helping people build a real relationship with money — without the guilt that usually comes with it.

Same method. Two paths. One common ground.
