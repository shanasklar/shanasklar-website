Meet your guide

Hi. I'm Shana.

I spent eight years as a nurse. I showed up. I cared. I worked harder than almost anyone I knew. And I was suffocating.

Not because nursing isn't meaningful — it is. But I had spent eight years being exactly who everyone needed me to be. I had confused dutiful with purposeful.

The day I walked away was the most terrifying decision of my life. It was also the first day I truly felt like myself.

Now I help women who are exactly where I was — smart, capable, quietly desperate for something more — find their way out. Not recklessly. Strategically. With clarity, with confidence, and with someone in their corner who's already walked through the fire.

You don't need to blow up your life. You just need a guide.
